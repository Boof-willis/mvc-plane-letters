Mountain View Commons
Heber City, Utah — Hwy 40 & Airport Rd
Live · Work · Fly

You flew into Heber City several times last year. You already know the area -- the access to Deer Valley, the Wasatch Back, and what makes this corridor different from the rest of Utah. Next time you're on the ground, there's something worth a closer look less than two miles from where you land.

Mountain View Commons is a flex space development at the south end of Hwy 40 and Airport Road. Each unit is three stories with approximately 5,000 square feet of space built on a 30' x 60' ground-floor footprint. The ground level features drive-through access with 16' roll-up garage doors on both sides -- ideal for vehicles, RVs, boats, collector cars, or building out a private workshop.

The second and third floors are fully configurable for residential or commercial use. Depending on the floor plan, upper levels can include 1 to 3 bedrooms, office space, a full kitchen and living area, or income-producing commercial space. Four finished design packages are available, each delivered with a certificate of occupancy.

Gray shell units start at $1,050,000 for interior units and $1,250,000 for end and duplex units -- ready for you, your contractor, or our build team to customize. Finished packages range from $1,900,000 to $2,450,000, coming in just over $400 per square foot.

With over $1 billion invested in the Deer Valley expansion just 15 minutes away and continued growth across Wasatch County, this is a rare opportunity to own flexible, multi-use space in one of the fastest-growing corridors in the Mountain West.

Come see the finished model unit in person. Open house hours are Tuesday through Friday from 1 to 5 PM and Saturday from 11 AM to 3 PM. Or call to schedule a private showing.
